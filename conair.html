Get to kerela by Air

Kerala can be accessed by air. The state has three main airports located at Thiruvananthapuram, Kochi and Kozhikode. Two of which Thiruvananthapuram and Kochi has an international airport which serves both major domestic and international flights all over the world, round the year while The Karipur Airport, Kozhikode present domestic airlines.

hiruvananthapuram, the capital of Kerala is connected to the important cities of India including, New-Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, and Mumbai. How to reach Kerala state by airways from these major Indian cities- It takes nearly three hours to reach the capital from Mumbai, Around four hours from New-Delhi and an hour from Chennai .Since there are no direct flights available from Kolkata to Thiruvananthapuram the quickest way to reach Kerala is to board a flight bound for Chennai from Kolkata and then take a flight to the capital of Kerala. You will be there in three and a half hours.
